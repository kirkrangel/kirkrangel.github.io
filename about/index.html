About Kirk Rangel

Kirk Rangel is a student in the College of Journalism and Mass Communications at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. He is currently earning his Bachelors of Journalism, majoring in Sports Media and Communication with a minor in Business Administration. In 2020, he graduated from Arlington High School in Arlington, Nebraska. He is currently exploring opportunities and career paths in photography and videography. He holds knowledge in the Adobe Creative Suite with skills in Audition, Lightroom, Photoshop, and Premiere Pro. He has gained experience through helping with StrivTv and the Social Media Team at Arlington High School. He is also creating an extensive sports photography portfolio covering events such as high school sports, the 2022 NSAA State Basketball Championships, and the 2022 Nebraska Football Spring Game.
